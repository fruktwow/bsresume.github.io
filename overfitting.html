Overfitting and Underfitting : Overfitting occours when the model is very complex where is underfitting occours when model to too simple. Overfitting also occours when dataset is small. Overfitted model works well in training phase whereas it works worst during testing phase. Underfitted model works worst during both training and testing phase.
